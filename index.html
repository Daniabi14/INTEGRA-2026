About INTEGRA 2026

INTEGRA 2026 is an inter-college symposium organized by the PG & Research Department of Computer Science at Don Bosco College (Co-Ed), Yelagiri Hills. This symposium brings together students from various colleges to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation through multiple competitive events.

The symposium features 8 exciting events covering various domains of computer science and technology, along with an overall championship event - Digital Don.

About Department

The PG & Research Department of Computer Science at Don Bosco College (Co-Ed), Yelagiri Hills is committed to excellence in education and research. The department offers advanced programs in computer science and provides a platform for students to explore cutting-edge technologies and research opportunities.

Our faculty members are dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering innovation among students, preparing them for successful careers in the technology industry.

About College

Don Bosco College (Co-Ed), Yelagiri Hills is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education and holistic development of students. Located in the serene hills of Yelagiri, the college offers a conducive environment for learning and growth.

The college is known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art facilities, and commitment to producing well-rounded individuals who contribute meaningfully to society.
